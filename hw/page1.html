This is a quokka!

Quokkas are basically cute, little kangaroos. They are marsupials and are around 15-20 inches tall. They inhabit Rottnest Island and are how the island got its name - the explorer who named the island called it "Rat's Nest" and while not entirely accurate, the name stuck.
