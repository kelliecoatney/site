So I decided to show you some of my favorite animals to show people!

These are blue-footed boobies!

Blue-footed boobies are medium size birds found in Central and South American coasts - most in the Galapagos - that make use of their colorful feet! They use them to attract mates by doing a high-stomp strut and when they have babies, the parents keep them warm by covering them with the webbed feet.
