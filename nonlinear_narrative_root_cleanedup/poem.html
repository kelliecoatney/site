Father

No words I write could ever say

How deeply I miss you every day

As time goes on I worry more

Of what continues outside this door

You left as swift as fall’s cold wind

The wound jarred enough to not mend

Amidst the pain I have great fear

Of deepening shadows I’ve seen come near

You gave me all to show our bond

But I bet you regret that from the beyond

There’s tension within this house of yours

I bet I have days left before it pours

The rain will come and when it falls

I’ll give the warning to empty the halls

I fear of a plot, a plot against me

To take what you gave and not let it be

So I’ll empty the grounds of the people within

And ensure the plot will never begin

A murder I think, is the plan I’ll upset

They don’t think I know, I surely bet

It’s a matter of days until I’m free

Once they leave, I‘ll be fine you see

Missing you aches, makes my heart slow

The writing helps and I just want you to know

You’ll always be in my heart and soul

I miss you , father
